You are randomly paired with another Prolific user.
You need to work together to decode an English word. Each letter is coded as a different emoji.
You only have half the letters/emojis you need to decode the word.
Your partner has the remaining letters/emojis.
Remember that neither you nor your partner have all the letters needed to decode the word.
Chat with your partner to exchange missing emojis and letter pairs.
You can only move on to the next task when both you and your partner submit the correct word.
For each task, you and your partner will receive a new set of letters and corresponding emojis, as well as a new word to decode.
