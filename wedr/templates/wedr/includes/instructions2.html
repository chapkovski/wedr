You will be paired up with another Prolific user randomly.
You need to work together to decode an English word. Each letter is encoded as a different emoji.
On your screen you have only half the letters/emojis of the word.
Your partner has the remaining letters/emojis.
Remember, neither you nor your partner has all the letters needed to decode the word.
Chat with your partner to exchange missing emojis and letter pairs.
The game will automatically advance you and your partner to the next task once both of you have submitted the correct word.
For each task you and your partner will receive a new set of letters and corresponding emojis and a new word to decode.
Your goal is to decode as many words as possible within the time limit.
