{% endif %} Dictionary: At the top of the screen, you will see a dictionary that shows the correspondence between each letter of the alphabet and a specific emoji. For example, if 😊 corresponds to 'A', every time you see 😊, it represents the letter 'A'.

{% endif %} Encoded Word: Below the dictionary, you will see a word that is encoded using these emojis. For instance, if you see 😊😎👍, and 😊 corresponds to 'A', 😎 to 'B', and 👍 to 'C', the encoded word is 'ABC'.
Input Field: You will also see an input field where you can type in the decoded word.
You will be paired up with another Prolific user randomly.
Decipher an English word, each letter encoded as a different emoji.
At your screen you have only half the letters/emojis of the word.
Your partner has the remaining letters/emojis.
Remember, neither you nor your partner has all the letters needed to decode the word.
Discuss with your partner to exchange missing emojis and share your emoji-letter correspondences.
The game will automatically advance you and your partner to the next task once both of you have independently submitted the correct word.
Your goal is to decipher as many words as possible within the time limit.
