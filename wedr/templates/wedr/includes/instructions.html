Instructions for the Word Decoding Task

Objective

In this task, you are required to decode words that are represented by emojis. Your goal is to correctly identify the word that each set of emojis represents.

How to Play
  1. Dictionary: At the top of the screen, you will see a dictionary that shows the correspondence between each letter of the alphabet and a specific emoji.
    • For example, if 😊 corresponds to 'A', every time you see 😊, it represents the letter 'A'.
    • Note: The dictionary will change for each new word.
  2. Encoded Word: Below the dictionary, you will see a word that is encoded using these emojis.
    • For instance, if you see 😊😎👍, and 😊 corresponds to 'A', 😎 to 'B', and 👍 to 'C', the encoded word is 'ABC'.
  3. Input Field: You will also see an input field where you can type in the decoded word.
Rules
  • You must type the word in accordance with the emoji-to-letter dictionary provided. Both capitalized and lowercase letters are acceptable
  • The program will not allow incorrect answers. If you enter a wrong word, you will need to try again until you get it right.
Tips
  • Pay close attention to the dictionary, as it is your key to decoding the words correctly.
  • Take your time. There is no time limit for each word.
Ready to Start?

When you are ready, click the 'Next' button to begin the task.
