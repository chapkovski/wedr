  • Thank you for choosing to support our research by participating in our study! On this page we would like to inform you about the study.
  • What this study is about:
  • In this study, we examine how people work together. To achieve this goal, you will need to work with another member of Prolific. Your task is to decode {{ num_words }} English words.
  • What you can expect:
  • On the next few pages we will explain in detail what you should do and ask you some questions to check that you have fully understood them. Then we will ask you to provide some general information about yourself. This is followed by the study, which consists of {{ num_words }} Tasks where you and your partner have to decode a simple English word that is decoded with emojis. After all {{ num_words }} tasks have been successfully decoded, a short survey follows and you are redirected back to Prolific.
  • Participation conditions:
  • Your participation in this study is entirely voluntary. There are no known risks or burdens associated with participation. You will receive compensation for participating in the study, announced on Prolific platform, once you have completed the study. You can withdraw from the study at any time without having to pay a penalty. However, if you withdraw before the end of the study, the compensation will be forfeited in accordance with Prolificâ€™s rules.
  • Data privacy:
  • Please be assured that all data collected as part of this study will be treated confidentially. You will be asked to provide some basic demographic information about yourself, but none of this information will be used in any way to identify you personally, and no personal information will appear in any published study.
  • Data storage:
  • Your data will be stored securely and we will ensure that unauthorised persons do not have access to it. Your decision to participate in this study will be kept completely confidential and only the researchers will have access to the individual data. We will not collect any data that can identify you personally. All demographic data will be stored electronically on a web-based server until it is downloaded for analysis. This server is only accessible to the research team with a secure password.
  • Approval by the ethics committee:
  • This study has been reviewed by the German Association for Experimental Economic Research (https://gfew.de/). If you are interested, you can receive an approval confirmation for this research study by emailing the primary researcher Dr. Philipp Chapkovski at filipp.chapkovskii@uni-due.de
  • Contact
  • If you have any questions, please contact Dr. Philipp Chapkovski at University Duisburg-Essen (email: filipp.chapkovskii@uni-due.de).
